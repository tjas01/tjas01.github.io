About Me

I help leaders and teams make confident, data-driven decisions. My background spans executive reporting, automation, data governance, and applied machine learning. I am Microsoft PL-300 certified and comfortable presenting insights to large audiences.

My skills:

  • Executive dashboards: deliver clear Power BI views with guidance notes and weekly readouts.
  • Automation: design ETL flows with Python, n8n, Make, and Microsoft Fabric that reduce manual effort and increase validation.
  • Governance and quality: establish data dictionaries, ownership, and refresh cadence; run recurring checks that keep KPIs trustworthy.
  • Analysis: SQL and Excel by default; BigQuery when scale is required; experience across supply chain, ERP, and IoT.
  • Applied ML: regression, clustering, recommender systems, gradient boosting, and neural networks for pattern discovery and forecasting.
  • Enablement: concise training and walkthroughs that improve data submission consistency and reduce correction cycles.
